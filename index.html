Amir-Hossein Shahidzadeh

PhD Candidate

Perception and Robotics Group - UMD

I am Amir-Hossein, a Computer Science PhD Candidate at the University of Maryland. I work with Perception and Robotics Group under supervision of Prof. Yiannis Aloimonos and Dr. Cornelia Fermüller.

My research focus is at the intersection of Computer Vision, Reinforcement Learning and Robotics which includes (1) perception for robotics, (2) state estimation and control, (3) sim2real transfer.

  • Multi-Modal Representation for Robotics
